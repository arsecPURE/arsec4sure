About Me

I am a passionate and seasoned Minecraft player with a proven track record of contributing to and shaping some of Nepal's most vibrant SMP communities. My journey is built on a foundation of collaboration, technical understanding, and a deep commitment to enhancing the player experience.

My expertise was honed on the front lines of Neporia Realms, one of Nepal's premier active Lifesteal servers, where strategy and survival go hand-in-hand. I've navigated the complex economies of Nepali Token SMP and Nepali Gem SMP, mastering server-specific mechanics and thriving within their unique ecosystems.

Furthermore, I have been a part of the entire legacy of Yeticraft, having played through Seasons 1, 2, and 3. This long-term commitment has given me a unique perspective on server evolution, meta shifts, and the building of lasting in-game legacies.
